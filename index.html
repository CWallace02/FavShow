About

A group of hyperprivileged Manhattan private-school kids seem to get away with everything. Except an anonymous blogger is watching their every move. Secrets get out into the public. Drama and romances are stirred on the upper east-side. The blogger is a mystery that these kids must find to put an end to the gossip.
