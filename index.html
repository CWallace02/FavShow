GossipGirl

About

A group of hyperprivileged Manhattan private-school kids seem to get away with everything. Except an anonymous blogger is watching their every move. Secrets get out into the public. Drama and romances are stirred on the upper east-side. The blogger is a mystery that these kids must find to put an end to the gossip.

Main Characters

Serena van der Woodsen is regarded as the "it girl" of the Upper East Side. Blair Waldorf is regarded as the "Queen Bee" of the Upper East Side. Chuck Bass the group's bad boy, frequently manipulative, and grew up as the only child of Bartholomew "Bart" Bass a self-made billionaire who owns the New York Palace Hotel. Dan Humphrey is a writer/poet, the son of rock musician Rufus Humphrey and artist Alison Humphrey, and is the older brother of Jenny Humphrey. Jenny Humphrey is a freshman social climber who idolizes Blair Waldorf. Nate Archibald is considered to be the "golden boy of the Upper East Side. The rest of their family members in the show are important as well.
